Dr. Mohsen Mahfouz

Designer of :

Documentary Historical Films & Events.

Expert in :

Reconstruction, Simulation & Revival of Ancient Egyptian Arts.

The History Egypt is directed by Dr.Mohsen Mahfouz, professor of sculpture at the Heliopolis University. He has experience across all aspects of production, from directing and design to management and historical cosultancy.

About Us

The History Egypt (History Production House ) is an Egyptian company working in the arena of mass communication, cinema and television. It is the only corporation in Egypt to specialise in historical productions for both documentary and drama.s

His team consists of experts, Egyptologists, researchers, technicians and craftsmen with more than twenty years experience making over a hundred films for movie and television companies including - BBC, Discovery Channel, National Geographic, History Channel, CH4 and shown across the USA, UK, France, Germany, Russia, Japan and Canada.

The History Egypt specialises in the history and culture of Ancient Egypt. It holds the largest collection of props in the world relating to this period and has a comprehensive reference library and contacts with every museum and historical site in Egypt.
